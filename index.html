Welcome to PROCRASTI-NOT

A website where you can procrastinate the right way!
Periodically schedule 30-minute study periods, and take 5-minute breaks in between.

How does it work?

  1. Click on the "Get on with it" button to start a 30-minute countdown.
  2. Time to focus on your revision/assignment.
  3. After 30-minutes, we will notify you it's time to take a break.
  4. Click on the "Take a 5min rest" button to start your break.
  5. It's a break, do what you want!
    (We suggest you scroll down to find classic games and interesting news feeds you can procrastinate on)
  6. After your 5-minutes is up, press the "Restart" button.
  7. Repeat.
